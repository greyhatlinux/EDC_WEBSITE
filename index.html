About Us

Entrepreneurship Development Cell, BIT Mesra has been providing a platform and mentorship to the students interested in Entrepreneurship. We Believe that honing one's presenting skills goes a long way. We believe in imagination and innovation. Entrepreneurship Development Cell, BIT Mesra targets fostering entrepreneurship culture amongst the students and establishing necessary support system for aspiring entrepreneurs. We aim to nurture innovation at the grassroots levels by facilating the interaction between students so that they can discuss different ideas.

E-SUMMIT

E-SUMMIT is the flagship event of Entrepreneurship Development Cell, BIT Mesra to foster entrepreneurial initiatives and activities in the campus. It is a unique platform for academicians, new age entrepreneurs, eminent business personalities, venture capitilists and the students to gather at one place and share their entrepreneurial endeavours and experiences and pledge to take entrepreneurship to a greater scale.

VSS

Virtual Stock Simulation is a one month one simulation game which replicates the stock markets. This game is created to give a glimpse that how a stock market works in the real world. This simulation helpls students get a bit of practical knowledge about stocks, rather than the theoretical one and help them develop their ability to analyze stock markets and it's prices.
